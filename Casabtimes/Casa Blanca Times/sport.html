    BRAHIM DIAZ, a brief about this Moroccan player and his football career.

    Ibrahim Abdelkader Diaz is a Spanish footballer of Moroccan origins. Ibrahim Diaz was born on August 3, 1999 in Malaga to a father of Moroccan origin (Safil Abdelkader Muhannad) and a Spanish mother (Patricia Diaz). He therefore holds both Spanish and Moroccan nationalities. He is the eldest of five children, and has four younger sisters (Zira, Idira, Dunya and Irina). He made his debut for Spain at the age of 16, receiving praise for his performance at the 2016 European Under-17 Football Championship. He plays for Real Madrid as an attacking midfielder in the La Liga. He previously played for English club Manchester City and Italian club AC Milan.

    His football career:

    Ibrahim started his career at his hometown club Malaga

    2015: Aged 16, he transferred to Manchester City for £20,000.00.

    On 21 September 2016, Diaz made his debut for City's first team, coming on as an 80th-minute substitute for Kelechi Iheanacho in a League Cup match against Swansea City. Five days later, he signed his first professional contract with Manchester City for a period of three year

    July 27, 2017, Ibrahim took part as a substitute against Real Madrid in a friendly match in the International Champions Cup

    On 19 December 2017, Ibrahim made his first start for the team, playing 88 minutes against Leicester City in the League Cup.

    He participated for the first time in the English Premier League on January 20, 2018, in a 3-1 win over Newcastle United.

    On May 13, 2018, he won the league champion’s medal after participating sporadically in four league matches during the season.

    On 5 August 2018, Ibrahim played the final 15 minutes in place of Phil Foden in the 2018 FA Shield.

    Brahim Diaz joined Real Madrid on January 6, 2019, for £15.5 million.

    Ibrahim played his first match with Real Madrid on January 9. He came on as a substitute in the 78th minute against Leganés in the round of 16 of the Spanish King's Cup, and made his first appearance in the league four days later, when he came on as a substitute in a 2-1 win over Real Betis.

    He played six matches during the league season, as Real Madrid won the La Liga title for the 2019-2020 season

    On 4 September 2020, Real Madrid announced that Brahim Diaz would be loaned to AC Milan for the 2020-2021 season. .
